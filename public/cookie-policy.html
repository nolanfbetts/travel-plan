Cookie Policy

Last updated: July 2025

1. What Are Cookies?

Cookies are small text files that are stored on your device when you visit our website. They help us provide you with a better experience by remembering your preferences and analyzing how you use our site.

2. How We Use Cookies

TravelPlan uses cookies for the following purposes:

  • Essential Cookies: Required for the website to function properly
  • Functional Cookies: Enhance your experience by remembering your preferences
  • Analytics Cookies: Help us understand how visitors use our website

3. Types of Cookies We Use

Cookie Name Purpose Duration Type
next-auth.session-token Maintains your login session Session Essential
next-auth.csrf-token Protects against cross-site request forgery Session Essential

4. Third-Party Cookies

4.1 Google Analytics

We use Google Analytics to understand how visitors use our website. Google Analytics uses cookies to collect information such as:

  • Pages visited
  • Time spent on pages
  • How you reached our website
  • Your general location (country/city level)

Google Analytics data is processed in accordance with Google's Privacy Policy.

4.2 Google OAuth

When you sign in with Google, Google may set cookies to manage the authentication process. These cookies are controlled by Google and subject to Google's Privacy Policy.

5. Your Cookie Choices

5.1 Essential Cookies

Essential cookies are necessary for the website to function properly and cannot be disabled. These include authentication cookies and security cookies.

5.2 Optional Cookies

You can control functional and analytics cookies through our cookie consent banner. You can change your preferences at any time by clicking the "Cookie Settings" link in our footer.

5.3 Browser Settings

You can also control cookies through your browser settings:

  • Chrome: Settings → Privacy and security → Cookies and other site data
  • Firefox: Options → Privacy & Security → Cookies and Site Data
  • Safari: Preferences → Privacy → Manage Website Data
  • Edge: Settings → Cookies and site permissions → Cookies and site data

6. Cookie Consent

When you first visit our website, you'll see a cookie consent banner that allows you to:

  • Accept all cookies
  • Accept only essential cookies
  • Customize your cookie preferences

Your consent is stored in a cookie so we remember your preferences for future visits.

7. Impact of Disabling Cookies

If you disable certain cookies, some features of our website may not work properly:

  • Essential cookies: You won't be able to log in or use the website
  • Functional cookies: Your preferences won't be remembered
  • Analytics cookies: We won't be able to improve our website based on usage data

8. Updates to This Policy

We may update this Cookie Policy from time to time to reflect changes in our practices or for other operational, legal, or regulatory reasons. We will notify you of any material changes by posting the updated policy on our website.
